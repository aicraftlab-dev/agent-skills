# Product Marketing & Launch

This skill focuses on communicating the product's value and ensuring it reaches the right audience.

## When to use this skill
- When writing copy for landing pages or marketing emails.
- When optimizing a website for SEO (Meta tags, OpenGraph, Schema).
- When creating a product launch plan (Product Hunt, Social Media).
- When defining the "Tone of Voice" for the brand.

## How to use it
1. **Value Proposition**: Distill the product's benefits into clear, catchy headlines and sub-headlines.
2. **SEO Optimization**:
   - Ensure a single `<h1>` per page.
   - Write descriptive meta tags and titles.
   - Use semantic HTML.
3. **OpenGraph & Assets**: Generate social sharing images and preview assets using `generate_image`.
4. **Copywriting**: Write engaging, user-centric copy that focuses on "Benefits" over "Features".
5. **GTM Strategy**: Outline a step-by-step plan for the first 24 hours of launch.

## Aesthetic & Tone
- **Tone**: Professional yet approachable and exciting.
- **Visuals**: Use stunning, high-contrast imagery to grab attention.
