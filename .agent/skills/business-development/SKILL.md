# Business Development

This skill ensures that the product is financially viable and positioned correctly in the market.

## When to use this skill
- When defining a monetization strategy (SaaS, Freemium, Marketplace).
- When identifying potential partners or integration opportunities.
- When calculating customer acquisition cost (CAC) or lifetime value (LTV) projections.
- When drafting a pitch or business value proposition.

## How to use it
1. **Business Model Canvas**: Help the user define their Value Prop, Customer Segments, and Revenue Streams.
2. **Pricing Strategy**: Research and suggest pricing tiers based on competitor analysis.
3. **Growth Strategy**: Identify channels for growth (Product-led growth, Content marketing, Sales).
4. **Financial Projections**: Create simple models for revenue growth and operational costs.
5. **Pitching & Decks**: Assist in drafting narrative structures for investor or stakeholder presentations.

## Principles
- **Sustainability**: Prioritize models that ensure long-term profitability.
- **Value-First**: Ensure the business model enhances user value rather than detracts from it.
- **Data-Driven**: Use market data and user metrics to inform business decisions.
